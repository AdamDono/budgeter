## 1. Executive Summary
**PaceDebt** is a specialized financial management ecosystem designed specifically for the South African market. While global apps like Mint or YNAB focus on US/EU banking, PaceDebt addresses the unique local challenges of **High Interest Debt**, **SARS Tax Compliance (Freelance/Side-Hustle)**, and **Local Cost of Living** (Fuel, Utilities, Load-Shedding costs).

---

## 2. The Problem
1.  **Debt Trap**: South Africans hold roughly R2 trillion in household debt. Most lack the tools to visualize the impact of interest rates.
2.  **Tax Complexity**: The gig economy is booming in SA, but tracking deductible expenses for SARS at year-end is a manual, error-prone nightmare.
3.  **App Gap**: International apps don't support ZAR nuances or local tax regulations, and local banking apps are transaction-focused, not strategy-focused.

---

## 3. The Solution (PaceDebt)
*   **Strategic Debt Crushing**: Built-in Snowball and Avalanche algorithms help users visualize exactly when they will be debt-free.
*   **SARS-Ready Tracking**: A dedicated module for logging business/freelance deductions, generating a "Tax-Free Savings" projection.
*   **Intuitive Budgeting**: High-contrast, dark-mode mobile-first interface designed for high-speed use on-the-go.
*   **South African Logic**: Built with ZAR as the base currency and localized categories (Petrol, Rent/Bond, Load-Shedding expenses).

---

## 4. Business Model (Monetization)
1.  **Freemium SaaS**: Free basic tracking; R99/mo Pro (Unlimited Categories, Advanced Analytics); R199/mo Family.
2.  **Lead Generation**: Curated affiliate links for Debt Consolidation or High-Yield Savings accounts (SA Banks).
3.  **Data Insights**: Aggregated, anonymized spending trends for SA retail/finance sectors.

---

## 5. Technology Stack
*   **Frontend**: React.js / Vite (High performance, SPA)
*   **Backend**: Node.js / Express (Scalable REST API)
*   **Database**: PostgreSQL (Relational integrity for financial data)
*   **Infrastructure**: Render / Aiven (Enterprise-grade cloud hosting)
